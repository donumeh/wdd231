About EcoRiders Club

Building a sustainable future through electric bike community riding and environmental awareness.

Our Mission

EcoRiders Club exists to make electric biking accessible, safe, and social while promoting environmental awareness in our community. We believe that sustainable transportation can be both practical and enjoyable when experienced together.

Our Story

2019

The Beginning

Founded by a small group of electric bike enthusiasts who wanted to share their passion for sustainable transportation and create a supportive community for riders.

2020

Growing Community

Despite challenges, we adapted to virtual meetups and socially-distanced rides, growing our membership to over 200 active riders.

2021

Local Partnerships

Established partnerships with local bike shops and city officials to improve e-bike infrastructure and access to maintenance services.

2022

Advocacy Success

Successfully advocated for new e-bike charging stations and improved bike lane infrastructure throughout the city.

2023

Education Focus

Launched comprehensive educational programs covering maintenance, safety, and legal requirements for e-bike riders.

2024

Digital Community

Launched our new website and digital platform to better serve our 500+ members with resources, ride coordination, and community features.
