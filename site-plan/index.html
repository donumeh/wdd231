Site Plan Document

By Emeka MacDonald Umeh

Site Name

EcoRiders Club

I chose "EcoRiders Club" because it perfectly captures the essence of our community - riders who are passionate about eco-friendly transportation through electric bikes. The name is memorable, easy to spell, and immediately communicates our focus on environmental consciousness and group riding. The "club" aspect emphasizes the community and membership elements that are central to the site's purpose.

Site Purpose

EcoRiders Club serves as the digital hub for electric bike enthusiasts who want to connect, ride together, and promote sustainable transportation. The site will provide:

  • Membership Services: Online registration, member profiles, safety pledges, and club rules
  • Group Events: Scheduled rides, route planning, meetup coordination, and event calendars
  • Educational Resources: E-bike maintenance guides, local cycling laws, safety tips, and buying guides
  • Community Features: Discussion forums, ride reports, photo galleries, and member testimonials
  • Local Information: Bike-friendly routes, charging station maps, and partnership with local bike shops

Our mission is to make electric biking accessible, safe, and social while promoting environmental awareness in our community.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: New E-Bike Owner

Question: "I just bought my first e-bike and want to join group rides, but I'm nervous about keeping up and don't know the local routes. How can I get started safely?"

Site Solution: The site will provide beginner-friendly ride schedules, route difficulty ratings, safety guidelines, and a mentorship program pairing new riders with experienced club members.

Scenario 2: Maintenance Concere e n r o wns

Question: "My e-bike battery isn't lasting as long as it used to, and I'm getting strange error codes. Where can I find reliable maintenance information and local repair services?"

Site Solution: The site will feature a comprehensive maintenance section with troubleshooting guides, battery care tips, recommended local repair shops, and a forum where members share solutions.

Scenario 3: Legal and Safety Information

Question: "I want to commute to work on my e-bike, but I'm unsure about the local laws regarding where I can ride and whether I need special insurance or registration."

Site Solution: The site will provide up-to-date local e-bike regulations, commuting route suggestions, safety gear recommendations, and insurance information.

Color Applications:

  • Electric Orange (#FF6F00): Accent color for links, hover states, call-to-action buttons, and highlighting important information

Typography

The typography system uses Google Fonts to ensure consistent loading and professional appearance across all devices:

Raleway Bold (Headers)

Usage: Main headings (H1, H2, H3), navigation items, and emphasis text

Characteristics: Modern, clean, highly readable with excellent weight variations

Open Sans Regular (Body Text)

Usage: Body paragraphs, descriptions, form labels, and general content

Characteristics: Excellent readability, web-optimized, friendly and approachable

Font Implementation:

  • Raleway: All headings (H1-H6), navigation menus, button text, and branded elements
  • Open Sans: Body text, paragraphs, lists, form inputs, and descriptive content
  • Font Stack: Both fonts include web-safe fallbacks (sans-serif) for maximum compatibility
